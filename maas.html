MAAS - Metal as a Service

DESCRIPTION —

MAAS (Metal as a service) is a web app used for performing actions on your server hardware. From upgrading or installing the operating system to creating a RAID is made easy through the UI.

The design challenge is making sure the information for each machine as well as the actions that are available to perform are kept visible and clear.
