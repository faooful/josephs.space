Hearthstone cards exploration

DESCRIPTION —

For fun I did a re-design for the Hearthstone cards. Using real playing cards as inspiration, I put mana, attack and health values in the top left hand corner so that key information can be seen at all times as if they were in your hand.

I also took full advantage of the beautiful art that Blizzard made for each card.
