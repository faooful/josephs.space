Hello, I’m Joseph — I aim to create what works well and looks good for a long-lasting solution.

I am currently a Senior Product Designer at Hologram in New York.

Prior to Hologram, I spent time at Google focused on improving their internal tooling for managing production at scale. Part of my time there was designing the tool all Google engineers now use to troubleshoot incidents.

Before that, I worked at Founders Factory, creating a new experience for podcasting. My goal was to validate the product for a founding team to adopt it.

When I’m not working you can find me me solving the NYT crossword puzzle, playing chess, or trying to convince my cat that if he talks to me, I wont tell anyone.
